About SafeHer

SafeHer is a women-centric safety platform designed to help in emergency situations. With real-time location alerts, one-tap emergency triggers, and quick access to trusted contacts, our platform ensures that help is just a tap away.

Our goal is to empower women with smart tools to stay protected anytime, anywhere. We combine technology with simplicity to create a responsive and mobile-friendly application.

Key Features

  • 📍 Live Location Sharing with Trusted Contacts
  • ⚡ One-Click Emergency Alerts
  • 📱 PWA Support – Add to Home Screen
  • 📢 Siren Activation for Attention

With SafeHer, safety is always in your pocket. Join our movement to create a safer environment for every woman.
